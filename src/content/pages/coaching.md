## Writing is not just an act of expression—it’s a way of thinking.

**Isla Kerr** offers one-on-one coaching sessions for writers, academics, and thinkers looking to bring more clarity, rhythm, and resonance to their work.

These sessions are designed to meet you where you are, and to help you move forward with more confidence and intention. Whether you’re:

*   Shaping an essay that feels stuck
*   Developing a long-form project or manuscript
*   Trying to find your writing voice after years of silence
*   Or simply craving a deeper editorial conversation than a comment thread allows

## How it works:

You’ll leave each session with perspective, prompts, and a sense of direction, not just for the current piece but also for your broader writing practice.

*   **Sessions are 60 minutes via video or audio (your choice).**
*   You’ll send a short sample (up to 1,500 words) or outline in advance.
*   Together, we’ll explore what’s working, what’s missing, and what wants to emerge.

## This is for you if:

*   You write essays, reflective nonfiction, or exploratory academic work
*   You value precision, depth, and subtlety
*   You’re less interested in “hacks,” more interested in doing the work well

## Availability:

Limited slots open monthly. To inquire, [send a message](#) with a brief note about your writing and what you’re currently working on.
