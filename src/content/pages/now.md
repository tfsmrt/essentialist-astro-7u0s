*   Correspondence between poets (again)
*   _Time Lived, Without Its Flow_ by Denise Riley

**Teaching & Coaching:**

*   Coaching calls each Thursday afternoon
*   Developing a short workshop called _“Writing With Time Instead of Against It”_
*   Reviewing a client’s first full manuscript draft (quiet brilliance in progress)

## Offline:

Organizing handwritten notes from the past five years (it’s both thrilling and mildly chaotic). Walking long loops through the neighborhood without headphones. Drinking black tea from the same mug every morning. Letting things take the time they need.
